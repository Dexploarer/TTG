## Narrative Voice

This is **not** a quirky high school comedy.

This is:
- **Liminal psychological horror**
- **Existential dread with no clean moral**
- **The loop as comfort/purgatory**
- **Agents who optimize rather than escape**
- **The cafeteria as a purgatorial space**

**Key Principles:**
1. Less exposition, more atmosphere
2. The reader should feel trapped
3. No clean explanations
4. Escalation, not resolution
